Bathroom design trends for 2025 according to Azzurra

The bathroom of the future is increasingly becoming a place of well-being and design, where personalisation and innovation come together to offer unique and functional spaces. The bathroom trends for 2025 see this room not just as a place for daily routines, but as a space that expresses style, technology and comfort.

Be inspired by Azzurra's proposals to design a high-quality bathroom in line with the latest trends.

1. Three-dimensional textures: the charm of Canneté and Rigatino

Textured surfaces are once again at the centre of the latest trends this year. Three-dimensional textures, such as those created by the Canneté and Rigatino processes, offer a refined and dynamic aesthetic effect. Symmetrical and asymmetrical Canneté is ideal for enhancing the fronts and sides of Azzurra furniture with vertical or horizontal lines that add depth and character.

2. Modularity and organisation with the Wally system

The Wally system by Azzurra represents a revolution in the concept of space-saving bathroom furniture. Thanks to its base units and wall units available in four different depths (17, 21, 27 and 36.5 cm), Wally allows you to create customised compositions that make the most of every centimetre.

The distinctive feature of the system is the upper tray shelf, perfect for storing everyday items, while the doors and internal shelves ensure order and efficiency.

3. Suspended design and visual lightness

The trend for suspended design and bases with minimal or almost invisible supports continues, giving the bathroom a lighter and airier look. For this latter variant, Azzurra offers solutions such as elegant feet or the new Atlas structure in aluminium.

The absence (or near absence) of floor-standing elements also makes cleaning easier and gives a feeling of greater spaciousness, making these furnishings ideal for rooms of any size.

4. Innovative, high-quality materials

The selection of durable, high-quality materials has always been a priority for Azzurra. Among the solutions available in the Azzurra catalogues, you will find these types of materials for designing endless combinations:

  • 32 finishes in 3D Decorative Laminate
  • 66 finishes in matt, glossy, soft-touch and metallic lacquers
  • 12 Unicolor HPL finishes
  • 10 finishes for wood essences

5. Technology and integrated lighting

The integration of technology into bathroom furnishings is one of the growing trends. Azzurra's proposals meet this demand with backlit mirrors equipped with integrated LEDs, mirrors with anti-fog function and shelves for charging smartphones. Solutions that improve the everyday experience by rethinking the bathroom according to the latest needs.

6. Custom-made bathroom furniture

The Azzurra collections stand out for their extreme modularity, offering elements that can be customised in terms of size, materials and finishes. Many of the base units, for example, are available in four different depths to create unique compositions that meet every space and style requirement. Equally interesting are all the wall units, which can be combined to design a bathroom that is customised in every detail.

Thanks to the wide range of finishes, each solution can then be adapted to enhance the character of the bathroom, transforming it into a true space of well-being.

The bathroom design trends for 2025 highlight the importance of a design that combines functionality, aesthetics and technology. From modular furniture such as the Wally system to the three-dimensional textures of Canneté and Rigatino, right through to smart lighting, the bathroom is transformed into a refined, efficient and highly customisable space.
