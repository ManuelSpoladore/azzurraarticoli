Lime 1.0: a collection featuring integrated handles

The new Lime 1.0 catalogue by Azzurra presents an essential and sophisticated vision of bathroom furnishings, where clean lines are combined with a variety of finishes, materials and opening systems.

Designed for those seeking a balance between minimalist design and distinctive character, Lime 1.0 offers modular and compact solutions that can be adapted to both small and large spaces, always with personality and elegance, defined by four different types of integrated handles.

Integrated handles: identity and functionality in a single gesture

One of the distinctive features of Lime 1.0 is the design of the four handles that define it, each with a precise aesthetic identity, allowing you to choose the language that best suits your style.

  • Nest is applied along the upper edge of the door and has a satin nickel or lacquered profile for a comfortable and discreet grip.
  • Jey is characterised by a recess carved directly into the door to create a visual and material effect of continuity.
  • Tratto stands out for its slightly raised L-shaped metal profile, available in various configurations.
  • Lofty creates a visually clean groove effect and is available in all 58 matt lacquered colours to match the same finish as the door leaf.

These solutions interpret the function of the opening not only in a practical way, but also as a true style element.

Compositions that express style

Lime 1.0 offers numerous bathroom furnishing compositions with a wide variety of bathroom furniture for every style.

Each solution can be customised with numerous types of washbasins with or without integrated basin, mirrors in various shapes and sizes, mirror cabinets, modular wall units and various LED lighting options.

Accessories such as towel rails and internal drawer fittings are also available, ensuring a comfortable and functional everyday experience.

Lime 1.0 is a collection designed for those who love visual order but do not want to compromise on the pleasure of customised details. The design of the base units and tops is essential but never banal, leaving room for personalisation through materials, colours and accessories.

Thanks to its modularity, Lime 1.0 easily adapts to master bathrooms, service areas or secondary spaces, always with the same attention to detail.
