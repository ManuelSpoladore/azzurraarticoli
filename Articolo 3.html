Azzurra looks to the future with a new look

In addition to offering cutting-edge bathroom furniture solutions, Azzurra is a brand in constant evolution. For many, 2022 has been a year of rebirth, and so the company has seized the opportunity to present itself with a completely new look, starting with its logo.

The restyling of the logo

More minimalist and elegant lines, with a contemporary feel: the new fonts used to compose the word ‘AZZURRA’ convey the spirit of the brand, with its constantly evolving bathroom furniture designs. The shapes have changed, but the style and quality, entirely Made in Italy, that have distinguished the company for almost forty years remain unchanged.

The new Azzurra website is now online

The website has also been given a complete makeover, with new aesthetics, functionality and content. It is a tool designed to bring us closer to our partners and customers, where you can stay up to date on the products in our catalogues and all the latest company news. It is a space to consult for all your bathroom design needs, facilitating the work of architectural firms and interior designers. It is a direct point of contact with the company for all your needs.
