Mini Washbasins: the perfect solution for bathrooms with limited space

The bathroom is an essential part of every home, but limited space can often be a challenge when designing this room. Fortunately, there are smart and practical solutions that allow you to maximise the use of small spaces, including mini washbasins.

The power of compactness

Mini washbasins are the ideal choice for bathrooms with limited space. Their compact size saves valuable space, allowing for better organisation of the room. 

Azzurra offers a wide variety of finishes and designs to suit any type of décor. If you have a modern bathroom, you could opt for a wall-mounted mini washbasin with clean lines and minimalist finishes.

The ultimate space-saving solution

Mini washbasins are designed with the specific aim of adapting to the smallest spaces, allowing you to make the most of every available centimetre.

When well designed, compact dimensions do not prevent you from obtaining the same functionality and comfort as any other type of washbasin.

In this solution by Azzurra, for example, the mini-basin leaves a small area next to the tap, ideal for a soap dispenser or sanitiser. Other personal hygiene and beauty products can then be stored on shelves and racks of various kinds organised at different heights on the wall.

Not only that, but the small size of this mini washbasin conceals a real treasure chest, as the space underneath the washbasin has been maximised to create a compartment with a door, ideal for storing items neatly and discreetly.

The charm of a mini washbasin

With this type of design and finish, the wow effect is guaranteed and this mini washbasin has nothing to envy larger solutions. In fact, the final effect is so pleasing that it can be chosen as a design touch even in spacious environments, coordinating it with other furnishing accessories to create an airy and relaxing atmosphere.
