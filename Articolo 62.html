Tips for cleaning bathroom drawers and wall units

In the bathroom, drawers and wall units play a fundamental role in organising the space, but to maintain their charm over time, it is important to clean them regularly using products that are suitable for the materials they are made of.

Thanks to the solutions offered in the Azzurra collections, you can choose from a range of durable and elegant finishes that require just a few important steps to preserve their beauty.

Daily cleaning: small gestures, big results

To keep drawers and wall units in excellent condition over time, it is useful to follow a few simple daily rules:

  • Remove dust and residue using a soft, dry or slightly damp microfibre cloth.
  • Avoid aggressive products: no abrasive detergents, solvents or rough sponges, which could damage lacquered or matt surfaces.
  • Clean any water or soap stains immediately, especially on more delicate materials.

Practical advice based on materials

The different finishes used on Azzurra furniture require a little specific care:

  • Matt or soft-touch lacquers:
    Clean with a damp cloth and neutral detergent. Dry immediately to avoid streaks.
  • Dekorativo 3D:
    Use a soft cloth, following the direction of the texture to preserve the aesthetic effect of the surface.
  • Veneered wood:
    Use specific products for wood or simply warm water and a mild detergent, always drying thoroughly.

To check how to maintain your Azzurra bathroom furniture, please refer to the user manual or contact your trusted retailer.

Internal cleaning: order and hygiene

The drawers and wall units often contain beauty and personal hygiene products: keeping them clean is also important for preserving the items they contain.

  • Empty drawers completely at least once a month and wipe with a damp cloth and mild detergent.
  • Organise the interior with dividers: accessories such as the internal organisation sets offered by Azzurra help you keep things tidy and make cleaning easier.
  • Avoid moisture build-up: allow items to dry thoroughly before putting them back in place.

Extra maintenance: when to take action

It is a good idea to carry out a more thorough cleaning from time to time:

  • Check the condition of the hinges and drawer runners (such as those on the Soft-Close drawers in the Azzurra collections). If necessary, lightly lubricate the mechanical parts.
  • Check that there are no water leaks inside wall units or suspended units, especially near the sink or washing machine.

Careful maintenance prolongs the life of your furniture and keeps your bathroom fresh and tidy.

Taking care of drawers and wall units means enhancing the entire bathroom environment, ensuring functionality and aesthetics. With the right precautions and products, Azzurra furniture will retain its beauty, continuing to offer style and practicality day after day.
