Space-saving tricks for the laundry area

If you have a small home or want to optimise the space available, one of the most difficult challenges is organising the laundry area in a functional and practical way.

Here we have put together some space-saving ideas to make your laundry room tidier and more welcoming, without compromising on style and comfort.

1. Choose carefully where to place your washing machine and dryer

The first thing to do is choose the location of the washing machine and dryer. If you have limited space, you can opt for a column solution, stacking the two appliances on top of each other and securing them with special safety kits. This will save you half the space and allow you to use the wall above for hanging shelves, cabinets or drying racks.

2. You can make your washing machine invisible so you can put it in any room

With a concealed cabinet, you can hide your washing machine and dryer when not in use and make them accessible only when needed. This will allow you to have a clean, harmonious laundry area that blends in easily with the rest of your décor. You can choose a cabinet with sliding or hinged doors, depending on your preferences and the space available: you will find lots of inspiration in the Lime Wash catalogue by Azzurra!

3. Make use of a hallway or unused space

If you have the opportunity to create a laundry area separate from the rest of the house, you can make use of unused space such as under the stairs, a storage room or a balcony. In this case, you will need to ensure that you have the necessary plumbing and electrical connections, as well as good ventilation. You can then furnish your laundry area with custom-made furniture that fits the size and shape of the space.

4. Organise every inch of space with the right accessories

Finally, to organise your laundry area in the best possible way, we recommend using practical and functional accessories such as baskets, boxes, containers and hooks. These will help you keep your laundry products, towels, dirty and clean clothes tidy.

With a little creativity and expert advice, you can transform even the smallest space into a functional and welcoming environment: ideal for making all your household chores more enjoyable!
