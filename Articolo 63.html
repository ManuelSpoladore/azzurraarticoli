Lime 2.0 by Azzurra: the throat opening that defines bathroom furnishings

In contemporary bathroom furnishings, finding the right balance between design and functionality has become a priority. The Lime 2.0 collection by Azzurra offers a concrete answer to this need, presenting a system that combines aesthetic rigour, advanced modularity and high-quality materials. A versatile, customisable solution designed to interpret the latest trends in interior design for bathroom furnishings.

Essential design with 45° throat opening

The heart of the Lime 2.0 collection is the 45° slanted groove opening system, an elegant and functional detail that allows for an ergonomic grip without the use of external handles. This element, which becomes the stylistic signature of the collection, can be made in the same colour as the fronts or in a contrasting colour, leaving ample room for design creativity.

The minimalist approach is also evident in the composition of the volumes: clean lines, balanced proportions and continuous surfaces that enhance every finish.

Wally: the boiserie system that reinvents walls

Another distinctive feature of the collection, a new addition to the entire Azzurra bathroom furniture range, is the Wally system, a modular solution that allows entire walls to be furnished with base units, wall units and columns with reduced depth, available in 17, 21, 27 and 36.5 cm. Designed as a truly functional boiserie, Wally allows you to make the most of blind corners and offers generous storage space even in the smallest spaces, thanks to internal shelves and corner solutions.

The upper part of the modules features an integrated tray shelf, useful for storing everyday items such as soaps, perfumes and accessories. The result is an elegant, practical system that can be perfectly integrated into any living environment.

Materials, textures and finishes: the freedom to customise

The Lime 2.0 collection can be customised with an exceptional variety of finishes and materials.

Among the most original are Dekorativo 3D Rigatino Tabacco and Cemento Tortora, Tekcolor Matt Castoro Ottawa, HPL Unicolor Pulpis Chiaro and the Blu Avio and Verde Salvia shades of Laccati, available in 58 different colours.

Additional design effects on the fronts of base and wall units can be achieved by choosing finishes such as symmetrical or asymmetrical, horizontal or vertical Cannetté. And for bathroom furniture with wood finishes, you can opt for solutions with grain direction change or front panels with continuous grain.

Why choose Lime 2.0

The Lime 2.0 range includes wall-mounted and floor-standing furniture, wall units, columns and bases for sanitary ware.

Choosing Lime 2.0 means investing in bathroom furniture with a consistent design, built to last and adapt to changing styles. The possibility of combining materials, modules and finishes makes every project unique and personal, in line with contemporary requirements for functionality, order and beauty.

Designing with Lime 2.0 is easy thanks to its intelligent modularity, ideal for master bathrooms, service areas or contract solutions.
