Bathroom design trends for 2023: 5 new features to keep an eye on

New year, new projects. What are the bathroom design trends for 2023? From established tastes to new design ideas, we have selected the 5 hottest trends for you, not to be missed.

1. A corner of well-being

For some time now, the bathroom has been increasingly conceived as a place to relax, where you can carve out precious moments of intimacy and well-being. The coming year confirms this trend, and bathroom furniture proposals are therefore enriched with new solutions, specially designed to achieve a perfectly tidy space that will reflect your inner balance.

2. Unusual shaped mirrors

No more classic rectangular shapes: the mirrors of 2023 feature contours with the most diverse geometries. Hexagons, ovals, with frames or special backlighting effects: like true works of art, they are the ideal starting point for decorating the bathroom wall with a personal touch.

3. Compositions developed in height

Among the new bathroom furniture compositions, we find an increasingly frequent trend towards verticality. The success of this solution lies in its advantage: it allows you to create an aesthetically more dynamic environment that is also functional in terms of space optimisation.

4. Confirm the suspended bases

Another solution that is now indispensable is suspended furniture, which continues to be popular this year. It is not just a matter of aesthetic taste, but also of practicality, as it makes cleaning easier.

5. Make way for bold colours

Less white and more black, accompanied by unusual and bold shades: this seems to be the summary of bathroom furnishing trends for 2023. However, neutral shades and nature-inspired finishes continue to be very popular.
