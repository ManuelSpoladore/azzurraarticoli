White bathroom: 5 ideas for a ‘total white’ look

White is a colour that conveys cleanliness, order and harmony. It is perfect for creating a relaxing and welcoming atmosphere in the bathroom, as well as visually enlarging the space. It adapts to different styles of décor, from classic to modern, minimalist to romantic. It also pairs easily with other colours, both neutral and vibrant, creating interesting and personalised contrasts.

Are you thinking of choosing it for your new bathroom? We have put together some inspiration for your project, all available in the Azzurra collections.

Sharing the bathroom in harmony

A solution designed for those who love sharing the bathroom with their partner, thanks to the base with integrated double basin top in white Tecnoril and the large rectangular mirror. The wall is embellished with full-height columns, interspersed with Climb open compartments illuminated with LED strips. The result is a natural and bright environment.

Dynamism and personality

Ideal for a modern and dynamic bathroom, characterised by an interesting play of volumes. The real star is the Milo floor-mounted washbasin in matt white Cristalplant, crowned by the Spectral Oval mirror with touch lighting.

Simplicity and style

Elegance and refinement combine different finishes in a cream white colour that is repeated in both the base and the wall units. The minimalist design of the furniture stands out for its long central groove, which unites and gives a sense of continuity to the fronts. The Calacatta stoneware top features the Tutto Fuori Unica Round washbasin in white ceramic.

Minimalism and functionality

Here, white creates a harmonious contrast with a few black details. The distinctive style of the Tratto handle immediately stands out on the white lacquered fronts. However, the real surprise comes from the wall furnishings with the Cube wall units in white painted metal. Finally, the floor-standing towel rail from the Look series is also colour-coordinated.

Classic style reinterpreted in a modern key

The double Slim ceramic console with a centred basin in a mirrored version is unified by the column wall unit, divided into an open section and a closed section with a door, also in white. A perfect interplay of shapes for a unique bathroom design.
