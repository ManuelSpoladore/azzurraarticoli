How to clean the mirror

If there is one element in the bathroom that everyone who enters the room is bound to notice, it is undoubtedly the mirror.

Yes, because it is absolutely instinctive for everyone to look at a surface that reflects their image. This is therefore one of the elements destined to take on the role of true protagonist of bathroom décor.

A large, bright mirror will give the entire room a more airy and pleasant appearance. To achieve this, however, it is necessary to avoid the accumulation of smudges and droplets and take the utmost care to maintain its beauty.

Does this seem like an impossible task in the hustle and bustle of everyday life? Don't worry, here are our simple tips for cleaning your mirror to perfection!

Cleaning your mirror in 4 steps

  1. Remove dust from the surface of the mirror with a soft cloth. This is essential to avoid scratching the glass or moving dirt from one part of the glass to another.
  2. Prepare a solution of equal parts water and white vinegar, or use a special glass cleaner. An extra tip: add a few drops of lemon or essential oil to give the whole room a pleasant scent.
  3. Spray the solution onto the mirror and rub gently with a simple microfibre cloth or paper towel. Be careful not to leave any residue, which could create annoying streaks that are difficult to remove over time.
  4. Finally, polish the mirror with another dry, soft cloth or a sheet of newspaper. Yes, this classic “grandmother's remedy” will help you easily remove all fingerprints and leave your glass sparkling.

There's no trick to it, just clean your mirror regularly following these steps and you'll keep it looking like new for a long time!
