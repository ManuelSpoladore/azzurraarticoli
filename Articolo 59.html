The handles define the design of the Lime Ø collection by Azzurra.

Azzurra presents Lime Ø, a new catalogue that redefines the concept of the contemporary bathroom, introducing fifteen different compositions, defined by the type of door and drawer opening and, in particular, by the wide variety of handles.

From simple functional elements, these become precious aesthetic details, becoming true protagonists of the composition's design. Materials, shapes and finishes for every style and taste allow you to customise your bathroom design to suit every need, both practical and aesthetic.

Let's discover the main features of the Lime Ø collection and how this new system can transform the bathroom into a unique and refined environment, maximising the visual impact of a simple and fundamental element such as the bathroom cabinet handle.

A minimalist design with maximum practicality

The Lime Ø collection stands out for its minimalist and elegant approach, in which every element is designed to combine aesthetics and functionality. The distinctive feature of the system is the introduction of a wide range of handles on the fronts of the base units and columns, a detail that adds value to customisation and ensures maximum practicality of use.

The Lime Ø bathroom furniture system enhances the presence of the handle, elevating it to a design element. The catalogue offers a wide range of compositions to choose from, featuring the Aster, Brio, Club, Delta, Diva, Fly, Gate, Grip, Lateral, Level, Like, ML, Simple and Stilo handles.

Added to these is the Push&Pull opening, because in this collection the handle stands out even when it is completely absent.

Handles with endless finishes for unique projects

The classic external handle is available in the Lime Ø system in a wide range of materials and finishes to add value to any furnishing project.

All handles can be positioned horizontally or vertically and are available in chrome, stainless steel, lacquered in 58 different colours, brushed steel, polished satin nickel and much more.

The endless design possibilities leave plenty of room for customisation to create a unique bathroom.

Additional finishes enhance the Lime Ø compositions

The Lime Ø collection not only offers a wide choice of handles as a distinctive feature, but also allows each composition to be further customised with exclusive details that enhance its elegance and character. Among the most refined options are the Cannettato and Cambio Senso Vena finishes, two solutions that add aesthetic depth and texture to bathroom furniture.

The Cannettato with its three-dimensionality transforms the fronts of the furniture into surfaces with a strong visual impact, playing with the rhythm of vertical or horizontal lines. The Cannettato Simmetrico or Asimmetrico creates a sophisticated effect of movement, enhancing the texture of the finishes and giving a unique identity to the composition.

The Cambio Senso Vena emphasises the natural beauty of wood grain or stone-effect finishes, reversing the direction of the grain to create a dynamic and refined contrast. This technique allows you to play with textures and light, giving the furniture even greater depth and a more intense perception of the material.

With Lime Ø Azzurra offers a bathroom concept designed to stand the test of time and adapt to the needs of those who live there. The possibility of choosing from a range of exclusive handles and materials makes each composition unique, offering a perfect blend of aesthetics, functionality and innovation.
