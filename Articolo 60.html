Why choose a sink for your laundry area?

Functional, practical, durable: the sink is an essential element for those who want a well-organised laundry area that is ready to support everyday activities. In addition to the washing machine and storage units, the sink is an indispensable accessory for hand washing, quick rinsing or cleaning bulky items.

With the Lime Wash collection, Azzurra offers washbasins with a simple, contemporary design that fit perfectly into even small spaces without compromising on style.

A practical element for every need

The washbasin is the perfect solution for hand washing delicate garments, rinsing shoes, cleaning bulky items, as well as being very useful for grooming pets or cleaning various types of sports equipment.

Azzurra models stand out for their design, which is designed to offer maximum practicality, and for the quality of their materials: deep, spacious tubs guarantee ease of use in all circumstances, while the modular structures adapt to any space.

Organisation and daily order

Choosing a well-designed sink also means simplifying your daily routine: having a dedicated space for hand washing, cleaning more demanding materials or simply for alternative uses of water (from plants to laundry) improves household efficiency.

In addition, the integration of drawers, pull-out baskets, shelves and closed compartments allows you to keep even the busiest rooms tidy.
