We are delighted to announce that Azzurra has won the Ecol Design Award 2022!

This prestigious award attests to the high ecological efficiency of our company, which has always been committed to good environmental sustainability practices.

The performance assessment was calculated based on five KPIs:

  • Reduction in waste production
  • Recycling of waste materials
  • Savings in fossil fuels
  • Carbon dioxide not produced
  • Litres of water saved

Thanks to this recognition, we are also helping to grow EcoBosco, the forest created through a collaboration between RECYCLA S.P.A., our partner in waste management, and Treedom.

By planting a tree from a distance, we support various projects run by small local organisations, combining environmental and social commitment in a virtuous cycle that benefits everyone.
