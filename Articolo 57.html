Guide to choosing handles for bathroom furniture

When designing a bathroom, every element plays a fundamental role in defining the style and functionality of the space, and handles are no exception. From simple opening systems, they are transformed into design details capable of enhancing the décor.

Azzurra offers a wide range of handles, including various types of integrated handles, with recessed and external handles, which adapt to different aesthetic and functional requirements. Let's discover the characteristics of each type of opening to help you find the most suitable solution for your bathroom design project.

Integrated handles: continuity and minimalism

Integrated handles are the perfect solution for those looking for a clean and essential design without visual interruptions. They are recessed directly into the door or drawer, creating an effect of continuity, and are available in four different versions in the Azzurra collections.

1. Nest

Nest is one of the most distinctive integrated handles in the Lime 1.0 collection. Inserted along the upper edge of the door, it features an elegant frame in satin nickel or lacquer, which guarantees a comfortable and discreet grip.

Available in a range of finishes, Nest can be chosen to match the front or in a contrasting colour to create striking effects.

2. Jey

The Jey handle stands out for its recess carved directly into the door, creating a continuous groove effect. This solution guarantees an ergonomic and minimalist grip, blending perfectly with the material and colour of the door.

Jey is ideal for those who love simplicity and want to avoid protruding elements, maintaining a linear and fluid design.

3. Tratto

Tratto is an L-shaped metal profile that protrudes slightly from the door, adding a touch of refinement. It can be positioned in different ways to customise the aesthetic effect.

This solution is perfect for those who want a compromise between practicality and elegance, without sacrificing the clean lines of the furniture.

4. Lofty

Finally, Lofty revisits the concept of a throat opening, but with a more decisive aesthetic. Available in 58 matt lacquered colours, Lofty offers a visual effect that blends in with the furniture, making opening simple and ergonomic.

Throat opening: the essence of modern design

The groove opening is one of the most popular trends in contemporary bathroom design. Thanks to a recess along the upper or side edge of the door, it eliminates the need for a visible handle, offering an essential and clean aesthetic effect.

The Azzurra groove system is available in several versions, including a 45° cut for a distinctive aesthetic effect, as well as an ergonomic and comfortable grip.

The groove can be chosen in the same colour as the fronts for a uniform effect, or in a contrasting colour to create a dynamic and modern visual effect.

External handles: practicality and elegance

For those who love a more classic or decorative design, external handles are an excellent option. Azzurra offers several variants that integrate perfectly with bathroom furniture for a customisable aesthetic effect.

The Lime Ø system by Azzurra offers 20 different types of handles in various materials and finishes to create customised combinations with the various front surfaces.

External handles offer several advantages:
✔ Wide range of materials and finishes
✔ Excellent ergonomics for a comfortable and secure grip
✔ Possibility of creating colour combinations or contrasts with the furniture for a striking design

How to choose the right handle for your bathroom

The choice of handle depends on several factors:

✔ Bathroom style: if you want a modern, minimalist look, integrated handles or recessed handles are the ideal choice. For a more classic or decorative effect, you can opt for external handles.

✔ Practicality: integrated handles offer a clean aesthetic effect, but if you prefer a firmer and more comfortable grip, external handles could be the best solution.

✔ Customisation: with the variety of finishes and colours available in the Azzurra collections, you can create exactly the style you want.


Handles are a detail that should not be overlooked in bathroom design, and Azzurra offers a range of solutions for every style requirement.
