Double or single sink: which one should you choose for your bathroom?

The choice of washbasin in bathroom design can influence both the functionality and aesthetics of the entire project. The decision between a double washbasin or a single washbasin depends on various factors, including the space available, the number of people who will use it and the desired style.

The collections by Azzurra offer versatile solutions for both options, allowing you to customise your bathroom according to your needs. Let's discover the advantages and features of both choices and which Azzurra models can best meet every need.

Single washbasin: simplicity and versatility

The single washbasin is the ideal choice for small bathrooms or for those who prefer a more essential and minimalist design. Perfect for a guest bathroom or for a refined solution, the single washbasin allows you to optimise space without sacrificing style and functionality.

The Azzurra range includes floor-mounted washbasins, monolith washbasins with integrated basin, all-out washbasins and even space-saving mini washbasins in a variety of sizes, shapes, materials and finishes for endless design possibilities.

Advantages of a single washbasin

  • Perfect for small spaces: thanks to its compact size, it leaves more space for other furnishings.
  • Greater design freedom: allows you to choose shapes and materials without symmetry constraints.
  • Easy maintenance: a single washbasin means fewer surfaces to clean.

Double washbasin: the perfect choice for sharing

The double washbasin is the perfect solution for shared bathrooms used by several people, such as couples or families. Thanks to the presence of two basins, it allows multiple users to use it at the same time, reducing waiting times and improving bathroom functionality. It is ideal for medium to large spaces in luxury homes.

Advantages of a double washbasin:

  • Ideal for couples and families: allows two people to use the bathroom at the same time.
  • More storage space: thanks to the wider tops, it offers more surface area for products and accessories.
  • Symmetrical and tidy appearance: perfect for those who love a balanced and harmonious design.

Which washbasin should you choose?

The choice between a double or single washbasin depends mainly on your daily needs and the space available.

  • Do you have a small bathroom or do you use it alone? A single washbasin is the most practical and elegant choice.
  • Do you have a family or share a bathroom with your partner? A double washbasin guarantees comfort and functionality.
  • Are you looking for a designer solution? Both options are available in sophisticated shapes and materials, thanks to the Azzurra collections.
