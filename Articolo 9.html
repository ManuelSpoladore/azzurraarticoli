How to clean the Fenix top

The opaque, soft-touch effect of a Fenix top is undeniably appealing at first glance (and even at first touch).

The choice of this material for bathroom furniture is particularly successful not only from an aesthetic point of view, but also in terms of functionality. It is extremely resistant, hygienic, fingerprint-proof and easy to repair in the event of minor accidents.

To keep it looking beautiful for as long as possible, simply clean it regularly, taking a few small precautions.

Here are some tips to ensure that your bathroom countertop stays as beautiful as the day you bought it!

Tips for cleaning Fenix

For daily cleaning of the Fenix top, there are basically three good practices and two actions that should be avoided at all costs: by following these simple rules, the result is guaranteed.

3 things to do

  1. Use any standard household detergent and apply it with a soft cloth.
  2. Always rinse thoroughly with warm water, removing any detergent residue.
  3. Finally, dry thoroughly with a clean cloth to prevent streaks and limescale deposits.

2 things NOT to do

  1. NO to the use of abrasive creams.
  2. DO NOT use abrasive sponges or hard bristle brushes. Instead, you can safely use a melamine sponge (commonly called a ‘magic sponge’).
