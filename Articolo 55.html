Wally: the space-saving system that combines aesthetics and functionality

When space is limited, the perfect solution comes from intelligent design that focuses on functionality without sacrificing aesthetics. The Wally system by Azzurra is an innovation that reinvents bathroom furniture, transforming every corner into an opportunity to multiply space. With its ability to create harmonious and space-saving compositions, Wally is designed to adapt to bathrooms of all sizes.

A modular system for maximum versatility

The Wally system stands out for its modularity, offering base units and wall units available in four different depths: 17, 21, 27 and 36.5 cm. This variety allows you to design customised solutions and make the most of even the smallest spaces. Thanks to the possibility of combining modules of different sizes, Wally is ideal for creating original compositions that optimise corners and enhance every wall.

Details that make the difference

The uniqueness of the Wally system lies in the details that make it unique. Each module is equipped with a tray shelf at the top, ideal for safely storing everyday items such as bottles, personal care accessories or decorative items. This detail not only adds practicality but also provides an elegant and functional space for your daily wellness rituals.

The fronts of the doors can be customised with different types of finishes, allowing you to create sophisticated combinations that are in perfect harmony with the other bathroom furniture components, such as the base units and mirrors. The result is a refined mix that captures the attention and adds character to the room.

Uncompromising space saving

Despite its reduced depth, Wally offers ample internal organisation options. The modules are equipped with shelves and doors designed to maximise storage space, allowing you to store items and accessories in an orderly and accessible manner. Furthermore, its ability to adapt to corner spaces makes it perfect for smaller bathrooms or complex layouts, offering solutions that do not compromise on design.

Aesthetics and functionality in perfect balance

With the Wally system, Azzurra offers a product that enhances the beauty of spaces without compromising functionality. Each composition translates into a perfect balance between form and function, transforming the bathroom into an elegant and welcoming environment.
