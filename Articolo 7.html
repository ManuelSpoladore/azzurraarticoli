How to create a laundry area

Does creating a laundry area in your home seem like an impossible dream? Here are some tips for setting up a dedicated laundry area, whatever space you have available!

1. Choose the ideal location

First of all, you need to choose the area where you want to place your laundry area. Ideally, you should set aside a dedicated room near the bathroom or bedroom or, for larger homes, in the basement. If you have limited space, you can still create the space you need by using a hallway or an unused corner of the house.

2. Check that the room is suitable

Once you have chosen the ideal location, you need to check that the space is suitable for a laundry area, not so much in terms of size (as we have said, there are functional solutions even for small spaces), but it is essential to check that there is a water supply and drain for the washing machine, an electrical socket and good ventilation to prevent the formation of mould and damp.

3. Optimise space with equipped furniture

It is also essential to choose furniture specifically designed for this environment, equipped with a range of internal fittings to neatly store the washing machine, dryer, detergents, laundry, iron and anything else that may be useful for everyday use in this room. Wall units, wardrobes and drawers should therefore be carefully chosen based on the available space and various requirements. Among the Azzurra solutions for the laundry area, you will find a wide variety of modular options to customise each project.

4. Consider adding a sink

The sink may seem like a piece of furniture from another era, but it is one of the most loved and used elements in the laundry area. It is indispensable in many circumstances, for example for hand washing delicate fabrics, but also for having a large space where you can wash bulky items or fill buckets with water for cleaning the house. The washbasins by Azzurra are available in a wide variety of sizes and with optional extras, such as a practical laundry board.

5. Customise the laundry area to suit your taste

Finally, it has to look good too! So consider all the options available when choosing the type of furniture, materials and finishes you prefer, so as to create an environment that is not only functional but also pleasant to live in.
