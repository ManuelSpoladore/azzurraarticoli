Designing a laundry room for sports enthusiasts: essential features

When it comes to a laundry area for sports enthusiasts, it is important to focus on functionality, durability and organisation. Sportswear often requires specific washing, quick drying and a dedicated space for storing shoes, accessories and equipment.

A well-equipped laundry room should include:

  • Dedicated spaces for washing and drying: washing machines and dryers side by side or stacked.
  • Practical work surfaces: large, durable surfaces for handling garments before or after washing.
  • Storage solutions: wardrobes, drawers and shelves for storing clean clothes, shoes and equipment.

Tips for the perfect laundry room for sports enthusiasts

Here are some tips for optimising your laundry area.

  1. Choose easy-to-clean materials: furniture with matt or moisture-resistant finishes is ideal for heavy use.
  2. Organise your space wisely: divide your cupboards into sections for dirty clothes, clean clothes and accessories.
  3. Create a quick-drying area: use telescopic bars or shelves to dry technical garments, which often cannot be put in the tumble dryer.
  4. Optimal lighting: a well-lit space makes everything easier, from treating stains to folding clothes.
