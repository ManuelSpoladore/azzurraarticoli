Guide to choosing furniture for the laundry area

Have you decided to create a laundry area in your home, but don't know where to start when choosing furniture? Here is our guide with all the tips you need to choose the best furniture for your laundry room according to your needs and aesthetic taste.

1. Initial planning

Before choosing furniture, it is important to plan the layout of the space carefully. Assess the size of your laundry area and identify your specific needs. Ask yourself if you need more space to store cleaning products and hang laundry or if you would like to have an additional sink. Good planning will allow you to select exactly the right furniture for you.

2. Durable materials

In a room where a lot of humidity is bound to build up, you need to pay close attention to the materials used for the furnishings. Laminate, stainless steel and treated wood are among the most popular choices because they are easy to clean and durable.

3. Functionality and organisation

In a room where order and cleanliness are key, space organisation becomes truly essential. Choose spacious wardrobes, wall units and drawers, also considering the addition of baskets of various sizes to separate laundry.

4. Sinks, washbasins and worktops

If space allows, a sink can be a valuable addition to your laundry area, especially if equipped as a washbasin. This can be used for hand washing, cleaning stubborn stains and making it easier to take care of clothes, shoes and particularly bulky items. A convenient worktop will also be very useful for folding clothes and as a support surface for everyday tasks.

5. Style and design

Don't neglect the aesthetic aspect: this is a room that will be part of your everyday life, so it's best to make it as pleasant as possible. Choose furniture and finishes that blend in well with your overall interior design style, especially with the bathroom, creating a total look from one room to another.

To ensure you design a laundry area that meets your expectations, we recommend consulting a professional interior designer or specialist retailers, such as those at Azzurra, who will be able to advise you on the best options.
