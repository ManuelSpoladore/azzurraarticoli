The elegance of corrugated patterns in modern interior design

Interior design is evolving rapidly, but some details seem destined to become timeless classics. One of these is undoubtedly cannettato (or canneté), a technique that combines classic aesthetics with dynamic modernity, finding its maximum expression in the collections of Azzurra. This technique, inspired by natural reeds, creates a three-dimensional texture that enriches contemporary spaces and sophisticated environments, adding depth and character to bathroom furnishings.

Cannettato: a stylish detail that makes all the difference

The interest in ribbed patterns, already strong in interior design, continues to grow worldwide. The uniqueness of this technique lies in its ability to transform flat materials into three-dimensional surfaces, where lines and shadows alternate to create a unique visual effect.

This style evokes Art Deco and vintage atmospheres, reinterpreted in a contemporary key. In the bathroom, the cannettato effect creates a refined and modern aesthetic that lends itself well to both minimalist and luxurious settings.

Azzurra's ribbed finishes: a world of customisable options

Azzurra's new Lime 3.0 catalogue introduces two types of ribbed finishes for fronts and sides, each available in 58 matt lacquer colours. These versions expand the customisation options, adapting to the most diverse tastes and styles.

Asymmetrical ‘A’ Type Cannettato

The three-dimensionality of type A lies in the arrangement of the lines, which appear in an asymmetrical pattern. With the option of horizontal or vertical installation, the Type A ribbed pattern allows you to create a dynamic and unpredictable effect, ideal for those looking for a bathroom that expresses originality. Each module thus acquires its own movement, a personalisation that makes each project unique.

Symmetrical Type ‘B’ Cannettato

The precision and order of the parallel lines define the Type B finish, in which symmetry creates a sense of harmony and essentiality. Here, the visual rhythm is marked by equidistant lines, which can also be positioned horizontally or vertically, making the ribbed finish a distinctive, sophisticated yet simple element. The result? A surface that resembles a modern bas-relief, perfect for those who love a minimalist aesthetic.

Why choose ribbed for the bathroom

Cannettato is primarily a style choice. The texture adds character without weighing down, a detail that enhances both small and large spaces, combining practicality and beauty. With its potential for customisation, cannettato is the answer for those who want a bathroom that combines comfort and design.

Choosing the ribbed versions of Azzurra means bringing an exclusive touch to your environment, a design that speaks of quality and authentic style.
