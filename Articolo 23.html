How to decorate your bathroom wall with wall units

To make your bathroom welcoming and functional, it is essential to take great care in choosing every piece of furniture. We are not just talking about vanity units, cabinets and drawers, but also everything that goes into decorating the bathroom walls.

In this sense, wall units are versatile and practical elements that allow you to make the most of the space, creating ad hoc and original solutions. Azzurra offers a range of collections to suit every style and need, which can be customised in terms of size, composition, materials and finishes to dress your bathroom according to your personal taste.

Maximum freedom of composition with Azzurra wall units

The Azzurra wall unit collections stand out for their remarkable modularity.

Each proposal can be adapted to the space available, creating tailor-made and creative solutions for projects that are always different and never boring. If you are looking for ideas for your next bathroom design project, you are in the perfect place to find the right inspiration!

Choose a touch of elegance with Cleo

Cleo is a collection of open wall units with painted metal sides that stands out for its three-sided opening and is available in both vertical and horizontal versions in different heights and widths. Its design allows individual modules to be combined to create a uniform effect. The range of finishes available gives these wall units an elegant touch, together with a final impression of great lightness.

Break the mould with Kros

An original, unexpected and totally different take on the classic wall unit concept. The character of Kros is immediately recognisable by its diagonal back panel, whose variation in width is followed step by step by its shelves, closed on the vertical side by a thin and refined metal side panel. Its ends therefore break all the rules, allowing the creation of original and unexpected compositions.

Create combinations for every space with Cube

30 cm high, 20 cm wide and three different depths: 15, 20 and 25 cm. With these simple dimensions, the small wall unit Cube is suitable for furnishing any wall. In smaller spaces, it becomes an essential storage solution and a surprising eye-catcher. In larger spaces, it can be the starting point for creating the most original compositions. Unexpectedly, the front is closed and the opening is on the sides, offering an optical effect that is always different depending on the viewer's perspective.

Unleash your imagination with Climb and Climb Free

Modularity and freedom of composition are taken even further with the Climb and Climb Free wall units, available in horizontal and vertical versions, the latter with two, three or even four sides that open. With a range of shapes, sizes, materials and finishes, there are no limits to your creativity. The wall will become a blank canvas for you to decorate according to your taste.

The combination possibilities are truly endless with Azzurra wall units because you can mix and match different collections and even integrate them with door solutions in a continuous play of solids and voids, following only the rules you decide.

All you have to do is start dreaming about your next bathroom furniture.
