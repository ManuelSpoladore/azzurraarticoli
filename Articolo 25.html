5 tips for keeping your make-up products tidy

If you think organisation and beauty go hand in hand, then you've come to the right place. We've put together some tips to help you keep your make-up products tidy and create a clean and flawless make-up area in your bathroom. Ready, set, go!

1. Every product in its own container

Using containers, boxes, baskets and drawer dividers is essential for organising your make-up products. These will allow you to classify your products by category, for example by dividing them into eye, lip and face products, making them easier to find during your daily beauty routine. The result will be a perfectly tidy space that is more pleasant to use and even Instagram-worthy.

2. Arrange your makeup and creams in chronological order

To prevent your make-up products from expiring or becoming unusable, organise them in chronological order of opening. Use the oldest products first (making sure they are still in good condition), especially those with the shortest expiry date, to ensure proper rotation and maximise their shelf life.

3. Pay attention to cleanliness

Wash brushes, towels, sponges and any other tools you use for body care frequently: this is important both to prolong their proper functioning and to ensure adequate hygiene! Take this opportunity to also periodically clean the outside of product packaging to prevent dust from accumulating.

4. Get rid of old products

Periodically, take the time to get rid of any expired products or those you no longer use. This is the best way to free up space, create a cleaner, more airy environment and keep only what you really need.

When you do this clean-up, remember to follow the instructions correctly to dispose of products in a sustainable and responsible way.

5. Create a travel-friendly kit

If you travel frequently or love to take your make-up products with you, create a travel-friendly kit. Fill a small bag with your essential products so you can easily carry them around without cluttering up your bathroom or forgetting anything at home.

By following these simple tips, it will be easy to keep your makeup products tidy in a harmonious and functional space. Organisation is the key to a flawless and stress-free beauty routine.
