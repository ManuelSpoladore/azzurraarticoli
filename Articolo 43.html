How to choose materials for the laundry area

To design a laundry area that is both aesthetically pleasing, functional, and durable, it is necessary to pay close attention to the choice of materials for the furnishings, which must be resistant, water-repellent, and in harmony with the design of the rest of the home.

We have put together some useful tips to help you choose.

Why materials are a choice that should not be underestimated

The laundry area is the room where furniture is most subject to wear and tear due to the use of water, detergents, steam, and heat. In addition, it is often a small space where many objects accumulate, making accidental knocks and bumps likely.

In such a busy place, it is therefore necessary to optimise space and choose the materials for the furnishings with care. These must be:

  • Resistant to wear and tear, stains, temperature and humidity changes, without deteriorating or deforming over time.
  • Practical, easy to clean and maintain, without requiring special treatments or specific products.
  • Aesthetically appealing to furnish with taste and personality, creating a welcoming and pleasant atmosphere to live in.
