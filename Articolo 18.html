Total black: how to design a black bathroom

Elegant, refined and chic: if the darkest of colours goes well with everything, there must be a reason. Increasingly trendy in interior design, the mysterious charm of total black invades every room in the house. But what are the aspects to consider when designing a black bathroom?

Let's take a look at how to create a classy and welcoming atmosphere with this shade, without risking a gloomy or monotonous effect.

The importance of choosing the right materials

The first step in designing a total black bathroom is to choose the right materials for the surfaces, starting with an assessment of their resistance, maintenance and appearance characteristics.

For unparalleled elegance, we recommend Fenix Nero Ingo, with a matt, soft-touch effect, for base units with washbasins. This finish gives surfaces a velvety appearance that is both pleasing to the eye and to the touch.

Combine it with wall units, mirrors and accessories with black metal structures, perhaps adding a few details in Laccato Metal Piombo to add a touch of glamour.

What lighting to use in a total black room

Black tends to absorb natural and artificial light, so it is important to create strategic light points to illuminate the bathroom adequately and, if desired, create dramatic effects.

To achieve diffused, uniform lighting, you can install LED lights recessed into the ceiling or walls. Add designer lamps as decorative elements to complete your bathroom décor (you will find various options in the Azzurra catalogues).

The most suitable furniture for a black bathroom

Of course, there are no fixed rules. The choice of furniture depends mainly on the space available and personal taste. In general, it is important that the countertop with the sink, mirror and wall units are all in the same style.

Among the Azzurra proposals, you can coordinate all the elements of the composition with the same finishes, without neglecting handles and accessories, such as towel rails. Particularly interesting for adding an original touch to the room are the ladder-shaped towel rails and storage units from the Look series, available in different materials, including black metal.
