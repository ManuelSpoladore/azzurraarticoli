Finishes for bathroom furnishings: which ones to choose? Colours and materials can completely change the look of a bathroom, even when the furniture chosen is exactly the same. That's why architects and interior designers know how important it is to choose the right colours and materials for the context. Some people rely on colour harmony, others seek a total look by considering the entire home, and others are able to tap into even the most hidden desires of those who are about to design a new home but do not yet have a clear idea of what they want. The possible requests are truly numerous and unexpected, with new needs, changes of mind or even clients always around the corner. For these reasons, it is therefore advisable to choose a bathroom furnishing partner that guarantees a wide range of finishes and materials, such as the options offered by Azzurra.

The infinite palette of finishes for Azzurra bathroom furnishings

Here, the wide range of finishes in the catalogues further multiplies the possible customisations of bathroom furnishings offered by their modularity. Let's take a closer look at the finishes available to customise various elements of the proposed compositions.

Dekorativo 3D

There are 29 different types of finishes available for this melamine, used to create structures, fronts, living room elements and tops, as well as for covering grooves. The 6 cm thick tops are made in the Dekorativo 3D laminate version. 

Lacquered finishes

The variety of matt, glossy or soft-touch lacquered finishes available from Azzurra can satisfy every need and taste. Used to create structures, fronts, living room units and tops, as well as for covering grooves, they are divided as follows:
  • 53 Lacquered Colours in Glossy, Matt, Soft-touch and Nuance finishes.
  • 5 Metallic Lacquered Colours in Glossy, Matt, Soft-touch and Nuance finishes.
  • 58 Soft-Touch colours: Very matt lacquer with a soft-touch surface finish, anti-fingerprint, low light reflection, scratch and abrasion resistant. 
  • 9 Soft-touch colours
    • can be combined with the same colours of the tops with integrated basin made of Fenix.
  • 58 Nuance colours: Matt lacquer applied to Dekorativo 3D Rovere Dogato Bianco 
  • 5 matt lacquered colours for metal elements (Cube - Cleo - Kros – Look – etc.)
  • 4 Metal Lacquered colours: Matt lacquer with metallic components (no heavy metals), applied by hand in several stages.

Unicolor HPL

This durable multi-layer laminate can be chosen for the construction of structures, fronts, living room elements, tops and tops with integrated sinks, as well as for infill panels and groove cladding. It is called Unicolor because all the layers are made in the same colour visible on the outside, thus obtaining a material that is even more robust than classic laminate. The Azzurra catalogue offers 11 different finishes to achieve an effect similar to wood, marble or mortar.

Fenix

As an alternative to Unicolor HPL, adding an additional surface layer with a matt effect results in the attractive and highly sought-after Fenix surface. This stands out because it is extremely opaque, with a soft-touch effect. As well as being aesthetically appealing, it is also functional because it is anti-fingerprint and easy to repair. Here too, there are 9 shades offered by Azzurra, each one more refined than the last.
