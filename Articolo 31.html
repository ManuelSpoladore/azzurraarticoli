The trend of compositions developed in height

In bathroom furnishings, the choice of vertical compositions is one of the most popular trends today. The success of this type of solution is probably mainly due to a perfect mix of design and functionality, as they allow you to make the most of vertical space with furniture and accessories that make the bathroom more practical and modern.

But why choose tall compositions for your bathroom? What are all the advantages and reasons that make this trend so popular and appreciated? Let's take a look together.

4 good reasons to choose a vertical design for your bathroom furniture

  1. Space optimisation. As mentioned above, the first advantage of tall compositions is that they are ideal for making the most of every available centimetre, especially if you are dealing with a tall, narrow bathroom. This allows you to optimise the use of the wall and, if necessary, create more floor space with wall-mounted furniture for a tidier, cleaner and more comfortable bathroom.
  2. Aesthetic dynamism. By focusing on verticality, you can create a more dynamic and contemporary environment. Play with the shapes, colours, materials and finishes of the various elements that make up the furniture, creating contrasts or tone-on-tone effects to achieve exactly the visual effect you want. For an original and creative effect, you can have fun combining different elements, such as columns, wall units, towel rails, shelves and storage units.
  3. Practical functionality. Tall compositions allow you to better organise your bathroom items and products, storing them in an orderly and functional way, depending on how often they are used. In addition, full-height cabinets or wall units make it easier to clean the bathroom, preventing dust and dirt from accumulating under the furniture.
  4. Personal style. Occupying a large portion of the wall, these elements stand out in the overall décor and are therefore ideal for expressing your personal style. The choice of materials, colours and finishes, as well as any furnishing accessories that complete the atmosphere, will give it a unique look.
